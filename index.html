Red Dead Redemption 2

Set in the late 1800s, "Red Dead Redemption 2" is an open-world game that takes place in a fictionalized version of the American Wild West. Players step into the boots of Arthur Morgan, an outlaw and member of the Van der Linde gang led by Dutch van der Linde. The story follows Arthur and the gang as they attempt to survive in a world that is rapidly changing due to industrialization, law enforcement, and civilization encroaching on the untamed wilderness.
